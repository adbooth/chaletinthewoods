About the Chalet

Our Chalet in the Woods is a two story, two bathroom home on 15 acres with fabulous views joined on 2 sides by Tioga State Forest Land.

Features:

Fully equipped kitchen with microwave

Fireplace and electric heat

2 master bedrooms (full sized beds)

2 small bedrooms (full sized beds)

Loft

Skylights

Cathedral ceilings

Covered deck with swing & large porch

TV & DVD

Gas Grill

No Phone

Bed linens and towels not provided

Children 6 and under are not allowed due to safety.
(The Chalet has 2 balconies, a loft, steep steps,
and no childproof safety mechanisms.)
